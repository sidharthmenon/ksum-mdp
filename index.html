Management Development Program

at

Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore

Kerala Startup Mission ( KSUM) in association with Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore conducting 5 day program Entrepreneurship Development Program specifically designed for Scaling up stage startup entrepreneurs from Kerala to help them have a better understanding of business environment and to have the right strategies, marketing and sales, fundraising in order to help them to become successful entrepreneurs.

Why should I attend ?

  • To have a deep understanding of business environment and to have the right strategies to become successful entrepreneurs.

  • Curated sessions about scaling up ventures from IIM Faculties

  • Insights from successful entrepreneurs in NSRCEL startup ecosystem

  • Access to NSRCEL mentors and and location visits

  • To enhance managerial capacities of startup entrepreneurs.

  • To develop and strengthen entrepreneurial quality, motivation, leadership and team management skills.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Technology/Product startups Incorporated in Kerala ( LLP or Pvt. Ltd or OPC)

  • Who is in the early revenue stage / having product market fit and is looking for high growth traction

  • Preference to startups who grabbed KSUM Scale up Grants/ external angel or VC investments

  • Must have DPIIT (Startup India) registration

  • Unique ID registration in KSUM portal

Who should attend ?

  • Startup Founders
  • CXOs

Program Fee and KSUM Scholarship

Total program fee will be Rs.1,25,116.00 per startup for 1 person for 5 day program inclusive of classroom instruction, training material, board (welcome kit, breakfast, lunch, dinner, tea/ refreshments) and on lodging in double occupancy. KSUM will be giving a scholarship of 80% for each startup upon successful completion of the program.

Startups has to pay Rs.25,000.00 as course fee advance through online payment

Type of sessions

Classroom sessions

The class room session will be Interactive sessions led by IIM Professors in IIM Bangalore campus. we kept participant number low as every one could get personal attention.

Panel Discussion

The sessions will have powerful panel discussions with successful founders from the Indian startups ecosystem and experienced mentors. attendees could ask questions and learn from the experienced persons.

Fireside chats

Evenings will be filled with networking and evening fireside chats with successful founders. This session will be great opportunity for networking and asking burning questions.

Meet NSRCEL mentors

NSRCEL is the incubator of IIM Bangalore and got well experienced mentors like renowned angel investor Nagaaraja Prakasam. The participant will get opportunity meet the mentors get advice from this mentors. followup session with this mentors can be arranged on demand

Location visit

During the 5 day participant will visit successful startup's location and meet the team to learn from their story.
